Haiti's Engaged Diaspora

Economic Transfers

The engaged diaspora plays a crucial role in economic development through remittances and financial support for local businesses in Haiti.

Cultural Links

With their deep-rooted cultural connections, the diaspora contributes to the preservation and promotion of Haitian culture worldwide.

International Networks

Through international networks, the Haitian diaspora fosters collaboration and partnerships that benefit Haiti's growth and development.

Skills and Investment

The skills and investments of the diaspora are invaluable for creating job opportunities and enhancing local capacities.

Strategic Role for the Future

The future of Haiti relies on the strategic engagement of the diaspora in various sectors, ensuring sustainable development.
