🔐 Security

Security Pressures in Haiti

The impact of insecurity on the daily lives of Haitians and the challenges of restoring peace.

Akèy / Security Pressures

Daily Reality

Impact of Daily Insecurity

The rise of armed groups in several regions of Haiti has profoundly affected the daily lives of millions of people. Economic activities, access to basic services and freedom of movement are severely hampered by persistent insecurity.

Trauma

Social and Psychological Effects

Beyond the material consequences, insecurity generates deep psychological trauma. The pervasive fear, the loss of loved ones and uncertainty about the future weaken the social fabric and the mental health of the population.

Displacement

Displacement and Social Disorganization

Hundreds of thousands of people have been forced to flee their homes due to violence. These internal displacements disrupt communities, disturb children's schooling and aggravate poverty.

Conditions for Development

Security as a Condition for Development

Without security, no sustainable development is possible. Restoring public order, reforming the judicial system and strengthening police institutions are absolute priorities to allow Haiti to rebuild itself.
