🌍 Diaspora

Haiti's Engaged Diaspora

Millions of Haitians around the world, ambassadors of their culture and an economic pillar for their home country.

Akèy / Diaspora

Financial Support

Economic Remittances

The Haitian diaspora sends more than $3 billion in family remittances every year, representing over 25% of the national GDP. These transfers are the primary support for millions of Haitian families and partially compensate for the weakness of public investment.

Identity and Memory

Cultural Ties

Despite the distance, the Haitian diaspora maintains strong cultural ties with their home country. Music, cuisine, Creole language, religious practices: Haitians abroad are active guardians of Haitian culture and its ambassadors in their host countries.

International Influence

International Networks

The Haitian diaspora, present mainly in the United States, Canada, France, and other Caribbean countries, constitutes a valuable international network of influence. It advocates pou Ayiti in international political, academic, and media spaces.

Skills and Investment

Return of Skills

Many diaspora members invest in development projects in Haiti and consider returning to the country. These skills acquired abroad represent a considerable potential pou Ayiti's reconstruction and development.

Vision for the Future

Strategic Role for the Future

The Haitian diaspora has a strategic role to play in the country's future. Its civic engagement, financial resources, and professional skills, combined with a national policy for diaspora integration, could constitute a major lever pou Ayiti's development.
